# Product Soul Schema

Full template for the `docs/product-soul.md` output file.
Read this when writing the document in Step 4.

---

```markdown
# Product Soul: [Product Name]
Version: [N.N] | Date: YYYY-MM-DD | Status: [Hypothesis / Validated / Confirmed]
Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD | Updated by: [skill / human]

> One sentence: what this product does, for whom, and why it matters.

---

## The User

**Primary user:** [Specific person in a specific situation — not a demographic.
e.g. "Freelance designer, 1-2 clients at a time, invoicing manually in Google Docs"]

**What they do today instead:** [Current workaround or alternative, and what's painful about it]

**Their words:** [How they would describe this product in one sentence — ideally verbatim from user research]

**Secondary users (if any):** [Who else interacts with the product and in what way]

---

## The Business

**Model:** [How money flows — subscription, usage-based, marketplace take rate, etc.]

**Unit economics hypothesis:** [CAC vs LTV directional view — even if unconfirmed]

**Year 1 must-be-true:** [The one business condition that makes year 1 worth it]

**Year 3 must-be-true:** [The condition that makes this a real business]

**Biggest business risk:** [The one thing that could make this not work from a business model perspective]

---

## The Strategy

**Named alternatives:** [2–3 specific products or behaviours users would consider instead]

**Why this instead:**
| Alternative | Why a user would choose us |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| [Alt 1] | [Specific reason — not "we're better", but what specific job we do better] |
| [Alt 2] | [Specific reason] |

**Unfair advantage / moat:** [What we can do that competitors cannot easily replicate, and why]

**Strategic bet:** [The one assumption that must be true for this to win.
If this is false, the strategy fails.]

---

## Product-Market Fit

**Status:** [Pre-PMF hypothesis / Early signal / PMF in segment / Broad PMF]

**Evidence so far:** [Specific behavioural data — not signups or pageviews, but retention, repeat use, word-of-mouth]

**PMF signal we're watching:** [The specific behaviour that, if observed at X rate, would indicate PMF]

**PMF threshold:** [e.g. ">40% of activated users return within 30 days without prompting"]

**Not-PMF signal:** [What would clearly indicate PMF is NOT there — the falsification condition]

**Segment:** [If PMF exists, in which specific user segment — not "all users"]

---

## GTM Distribution

**How the first user finds us:** [Specific channel and mechanism — not "social media", but "developer Twitter via specific pain-point posts"]

**Wedge channel:** [The one distribution channel that works before scale — and why this one]

**Acquisition → Activation → Retention loop:**
```
[Acquisition mechanism]
    ↓
[Activation moment — the first time the user gets value]
    ↓
[Retention behaviour — what they do that signals they'll stay]
    ↓
[Expansion / referral loop — how it grows from one user]
```

**GTM motion:** [Self-serve / sales-assisted / PLG / community-led / content-led]

**First 100 users:** [How specifically — not "grow organically", but the exact tactic]

---

## Open Hypotheses

Items marked (H) are unconfirmed hypotheses. Must be resolved before treating this section as strategy.

- [ ] (H) [Hypothesis 1 — what must be validated, and how]
- [ ] (H) [Hypothesis 2]

---

## Decision Log

Significant decisions informed by this document:
| Date | Decision | Section referenced |
|------|----------|--------------------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | [What was decided] | [User / Business / Strategy / PMF / GTM] |
```
